Build the next-play queue your way

Manual entries give you precise control. The automatic queue follows the cards remaining after your filters are applied.

  • Drag cards or exact clips into the manual queue.
  • Reorder entries or drag them out to remove them.
  • Save and reload manual queues as .iqpq files.
